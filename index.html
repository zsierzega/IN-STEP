IN-STEP Summer Institute: Monday-Thursday, 27-30 July 2020!
Entirely online four-day event! National Louis University and Illinois Institute of Technology announce a four-day experience available for Chicago Public School elementary and middle-school teachers and administrators. The goal is to acquaint the participants with the realities of scientific research and help them develop meaningful curricular materials for their classrooms.
